About

Sophie Bushman is a web designer from Denver, Colorado. She currently resides in Brookyln, New York. She started coding in high school. Then she studied marketing for a semester of college. Then she studied web design at the New York Code and Design Academy. That's what she's doing now.

Sophie mostly freelances. But that's not a stable income and she has cats to provide for (and an overly dependent relationship with seamless), so she took a job as the content and web assistant for blogger Eva Amurri Martino. She gets to work from home and eat pizza in bed all day while making pretty websites and creating super cool code.
